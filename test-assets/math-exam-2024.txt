MATHEMATICS BAC PREP - EXAM 2024
================================

Section A: Analysis (8 points)

Exercise 1 (4 pts):
Let f(x) = x³ - 3x² + 2x + 1

a) Study the variations of f on [-1, 4]
b) Determine the equation of the tangent at x = 1
c) Calculate the integral of f from 0 to 2

Exercise 2 (4 pts):
Let (un) be the sequence defined by u0 = 2, un+1 = (3un + 4) / (un + 2)

a) Show that (un) is bounded
b) Study the monotonicity of (un)
c) Determine lim(un)

Section B: Geometry (6 points)

Exercise 3 (6 pts):
In the space with orthonormal frame (O, i, j, k):
A(1, 0, 2), B(3, 1, -1), C(0, 2, 1)

a) Calculate AB × AC
b) Determine the equation of plane (ABC)
c) Calculate the area of triangle ABC

Section C: Probability (6 points)

Exercise 4 (6 pts):
A bag contains 3 red balls, 5 blue balls, 2 green balls.
Two balls are drawn without replacement.

a) P(both red)
b) P(at least one blue)
c) E(X) where X = number of blue balls drawn

Duration: 3 hours
Calculator: allowed (non-programmable)
